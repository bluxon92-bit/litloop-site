About Litloop

Litloop is being built for readers who want more than a shelf. It's for the person who finishes a book at midnight and desperately needs to talk to someone about it. For the friend who always has a recommendation but never finds out if you actually read it. For the book club whose best conversations happen in the car on the way home.

We believe books are fundamentally social — they're meant to be shared, discussed, recommended, and argued over. Litloop gives you the tools to do that with the people who matter most: your friends.

The blog

This site is also home to a reading blog covering fantasy, sci-fi, horror, and more. You'll find honest reviews, author guides, and reading lists. It grew out of Cabbage Tree Books, a book recommendation site we ran for several years — all that content has moved here.
