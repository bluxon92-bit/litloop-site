Writing YA that works

  • YA protagonists have agency — they make choices that drive the plot, not just react to it
  • Don't condescend — teenagers are reading for truth, not reassurance
  • The internal stakes need to feel as high as the external ones
  • Voice is everything in YA — get the narrator's voice right before anything else
  • The best YA doesn't talk down to its readers. It trusts them completely.
