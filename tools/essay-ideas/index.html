Writing non-fiction that works

  • The argument should be specific enough to be wrong — vague arguments are unfalsifiable and uninteresting
  • The personal essay isn't about you — it's about the thing you're using yourself to examine
  • In reported pieces, the subject who resists the story is usually the most important one
  • A good travel piece makes you feel the strangeness of the familiar, not just the strangeness of the foreign
  • Memoir is most honest when it admits what the writer can't know for certain
