Writing contemporary fiction that works

  • The external plot should mirror the internal one — what's happening outside should pressure what's happening inside
  • Contemporary fiction lives in specific detail — the brand, the neighbourhood, the year all matter
  • What characters don't say is often more revealing than what they do
  • Give minor characters enough specificity that they feel like they have lives off the page
  • The ending doesn't need resolution — it needs a shift, however small
