Features — Coming soon

Everything a reading app should do.

And the bits that most of them miss. Litloop does more than tell you how many books you've read this year, it also asks: who did you talk to about them?

Book tracker & reading log · Personal recommendations · Private book chats · Book clubs · Reading stats

01 — Track

Your reading life, all in one place.

Log every book, set goals, and build the perfect TBR pile. Litloop is the book tracker that keeps you honest — and keeps your friends in the loop.

01

Reading log

Log every book you're reading, have read, or want to read. Your full reading history in one place, no spreadsheet required.

02

Reading goals

Set an annual reading goal and track your progress through the year. Watch your 1/12 become 12/12.

03

Reading stats

See your reading habits at a glance, including books per month, favourite genres and authors, and average ratings.

02 — Connect

See what your friends are reading.

A real-time feed of the people in your life. See what they're reading right now, what they finished, and what they thought. No algorithm. No strangers.

Friends' reading feed

Your feed shows what the people you trust are reading right now, leave reviews, and share updates and you progress through your read.

Friend profiles

Every reader has a profile showing their current read, recent reviews, and favourite books. A proper snapshot of someone's reading life.

03 — Recommend

Send a rec. Close the loop.

Not a link. A proper recommendation. Book, note, and a reason. Sent directly to a friend. The digital equivalent of pressing a book into someone's hands.

Personal recommendations

Send a book to a friend with a personal note — not just a link, but a reason. All your incoming recs live in one place so you never lose one again.

Track whether your recs land

See when a friend picks up a book you recommended. No more "oh I meant to read that" — the loop actually closes.

04 — Converse

Have the conversations books deserve.

Private chats, book club threads, reviews and comments. Talk freely without performing for an audience of thousands.

Private book chats

Start a private chat about any book — just you and a friend, or a small group. Say what you actually think.

Book club tools

Running a book club? Start a group chat, share thoughts by chapter, and keep conversations going between meetings.

Reviews & comments

Leave a review when you finish a book. Friends can like and comment. Get honest reactions from people you know.
